Job Title                                Financial Reporting Manager
 
Job Description
Key Duties:
-Oversight of balance sheet reconciliation process
-Internal audit function with scope to recommend process changes
-Preparation of year end audit file and responsible for audit process at year end
-Legal entity control for subsidiary company of the group
-Assistance with annual budgeting process
-Oversight of reporting over budget performance
-Staff Management
 
Skills Required
-CA (SA) with articles and at least 1 year commercial experience
-Deadline driven and hard working
-Analytical skillset with the ability to identify and solve problems
-Proficient on MS Excel
